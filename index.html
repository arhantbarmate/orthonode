About Orthonode™

Orthonode™ is a specialized engineering brand focused on the research and deployment of zero-trust hardware gateways for decentralized physical infrastructure networks (DePIN). Our mission is to decouple hardware-level truth from cloud dependencies—ensuring that decentralized networks remain resilient, verifiable, and secure at the edge through deterministic execution.

Orthonode™ operates as a technical initiative of Orthonode Infrastructure Labs Private Limited, an R&D-focused legal entity based in Madhya Pradesh, India, dedicated to advancing edge verification systems, protocol research, and the Orthonode execution framework.
