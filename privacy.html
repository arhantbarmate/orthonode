Data Sovereignty

Effective Date: February 2026

Jurisdiction: Orthonode Infrastructure Labs Private Limited (India)

1. Data Lifecycle & Retention

Telemetry data provided for verification is processed in volatile memory or isolated compute environments. Raw coordinate data is automatically deleted within a maximum retention window of 30 days, unless a shorter period is contractually agreed. We do not retain historical route maps of Client assets.

2. Data Isolation

All verification workloads run in isolated containers. Data from one Client is never cross-referenced or mixed with data from another Client to prevent competitive leakage and ensure integrity of the forensic audit.

3. Minimal Input & PII Exclusion

The OAP verification engine requires only telemetric primitives (Time, GPS Lat/Long, Speed, IMU/Accelerometer). We do not require, request, or store PII (Personally Identifiable Information) such as driver names, vehicle license plates, or financial records.

4. Regulatory Compliance

Orthonode Infrastructure Labs complies with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP) 2023 of India. The Lab does not intentionally process personal data. Any incidental personal data present in telemetry logs is processed solely for fraud detection and integrity verification under legitimate interest exemptions and is not retained.
