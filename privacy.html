Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 31, 2026

Jurisdiction: Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, India

1. Data Sovereignty

Orthonode™ adheres to a strict "Zero-Knowledge Ingress" policy. The Orthonode Protocol is designed to validate hardware signatures without retaining user-identifiable metadata. We do not store GPS history, earnings data, or private keys on our central servers.

2. Entity Distinction

Orthonode™ is a specialized engineering brand and a technical initiative of Orthonode Infrastructure Labs Private Limited.

3. Telemetry & Diagnostics

Orthonode Nodes may transmit anonymous "Heartbeat" signals to verify uptime. This data is strictly limited to: Device ID (Hash), Uptime Seconds, and Firmware Version.

4. Regulatory Compliance

As an entity registered in India, we comply with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP), ensuring that any incidental data collection is minimal and processed solely for technical validation.
